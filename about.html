About CodeBashStat

I am Bree: paramedic, instructor, programmer in progress. I spent years memorizing drug doses, trauma scores, and protocols. Now I use that same pattern-driven brain to learn code and explain it to others.

Why this exists

Most docs read like they were written for robots. Most tutorials assume free time you do not have. CodeBashStat exists to turn “I do not get it” into “I see the system now.”

What you can expect

  • Short, focused breakdowns of real tools and concepts.
  • Side-by-side “original doc vs decoded doc.”
  • Labs where you fix or refactor code with guidance.
  • Honest talk about switching careers with no safety net.
